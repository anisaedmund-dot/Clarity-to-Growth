Ari Learns Series

Ari Learns is a delightful children’s story series that follows Ari, a curious and spirited little girl, as she discovers life lessons through everyday family moments. With the support of her big sister Isis and their loving mom, Ari learns about responsibility, kindness, teamwork, and problem-solving in ways that feel both fun and relatable for young readers.

Ari and Her Magic Sticker (Book 1)

One sunny afternoon, Ari learns an important lesson about car park safety after she dashes away from the car too quickly. With her caring mum by her side, Ari discovers the Magic Car Sticker rule and learns how independence and safety can go hand in hand.
